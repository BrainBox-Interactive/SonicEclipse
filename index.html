Welcome to Sonic Eclipse!

Sonic Eclipse is a fan-made Sonic the Hedgehog game that is currently in development.
The game is being developed by a small team of Sonic fans who are passionate about the series.
Our goal is to create a fun and exciting game that captures the spirit of the classic Sonic games while also introducing new and modern mechanics.
Stay tuned for updates and announcements about the game's development!
